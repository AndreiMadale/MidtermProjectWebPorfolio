What Does it Mean to be a Car Enthusiast?

Brian Alexander Tan
Information Technology Student of USTP CDO

A car enthusiast is someone who loves cars and everything about them; enjoys buying them, collecting them,
working on them, modifying them and anything else that comes to mind.
We love performance,style, handling, horsepower, speed,
even the smell and sound of them.

Matthew Erwin Batolina
IT Student of XU CDO

Being a car enthusiast means sharing a certain love for something that not everyone would normally likely relate to.
There’s just something very special about the suave curvatures and dips in the design of what makes a car
what it is. Not to mention, the way it just sings when purring out it’s regal exhaust notes.
But most importantly, a car enthusiast is all in it for the thrill. The thrill of
flying through speed. Speed is at the heart of every car enthusiast, and
that’s all that matters.
